Our waxes:

image courtesy of Matt Banks of at FreeDigitalPhotos.net

Rapeseed Wax - Manufactured from renewable sources from within the European Union. Rapeseed is a bright yellow flowering plant from the mustard or cabbage family that is a sustainable, renewable, greener alternative to petroleum derived products with a significantly reduced carbon footprint

Soy Wax - Manufactured in the United States. Soy wax is renewable, sustainable and biodegradable. Soy is a resource that can be replaced at a rate comparable or faster to the rate of consumption. Kosher Certified.

Our frangrances:

image courtesy of Praisaeng at FreeDigitalPhotos.net

Made in the E.U for use in natural waxes and carefully chosen to enhance your environment these fragrances are Phthalate free. Phthalates are used to dissolve fragrance raw materials when making fragrance oils.Recently, there have been concerns that high levels of Phthalates can cause health issues.
