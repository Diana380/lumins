We work from our home in the seaside town of Deal which is on the Kent Coast, here we develop our products to bring you candles that are good to look at and a joy to burn. We have chosen to use wax from renewable sources and fragrances that are kinder to the environment.

Our fragrances are made in the E.U for use in natural waxes and carefully chosen to enhance your environment.

Our essential oils are chosen to fill your senses with a delicate aroma that is natural and therapeutic.
